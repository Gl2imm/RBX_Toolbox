Import from Roblox

Pull player avatars and accessories straight into Blender — no manual exporting.

Accessory bounds

Live boundary checking so your UGC accessories pass Roblox's size constraints first try.

Rigs & templates

Spawn dummies, armatures and R6 rigs from presets, then animation-test your build.

Layered clothing

Ready-made cages and accessory samples to get layered cloth right without the guesswork.

Head and mesh templates plus a dedicated texture shader, built for Roblox hair.
